PrivaCity chatbot Game

PrivaCity is a chatbot serious game to teach players about smart cities, and the associated privacy risks. The target group is Norwegian teenagers.

The game is divided into 6 levels, where the player can explore each level by using a combination of commands and buttons i.e quick-replies. Before starting the game, there is a brief introduction as to what a smart city is, and how to play the game.

The game is available on Facebook Messenger
